Work Fields

IoT Systems

IoT refers to a network of physical objects or "things" that are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies to collect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the internet. These connected devices can range from everyday household items like thermostats and refrigerators to industrial machinery, vehicles, and even wearable devices. IoT systems enable these objects to interact, share information, and perform tasks without direct human intervention.

EM Simulation & Analysis

Electromagnetic (EM) simulation and analysis is a computational technique used in engineering, physics, and other fields to study and predict the behavior of electromagnetic fields and waves in various devices, structures, and materials. This process involves the use of computer software and mathematical models to simulate and analyze how electromagnetic waves interact with objects, guiding the design, optimization, and understanding of electromagnetic systems.

PCB Design

PCB design, or Printed Circuit Board design, is the process of creating the layout and schematics for a printed circuit board that serves as the foundation for electronic devices and systems. PCBs are essential components of modern electronics, providing a platform for connecting and interconnecting various electronic components such as integrated circuits (ICs), resistors, capacitors, and connectors.

Wireless Communications Design

Wireless communications design refers to the process of creating and optimizing wireless communication systems and networks that enable the transmission of data, voice, and other information over the airwaves without the need for physical cables or wired connections. It encompasses a wide range of technologies, protocols, and techniques to ensure reliable and efficient wireless communication.

Cloud Development

Cloud development refers to the process of designing, building, deploying, and managing applications and services that run on cloud computing platforms. Cloud development leverages the resources and services provided by cloud service providers, such as Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), and others, to create scalable, flexible, and cost-effective software solutions.

Data Analisys

Data analysis is the process of inspecting, cleaning, transforming, and interpreting data to discover meaningful insights, patterns, trends, and information that can inform decision-making and solve problems. It is a fundamental step in the broader field of data science and is used across various industries and domains to extract knowledge from data.

AI Object Recognition

AI object recognition, also known as computer vision object recognition, is a subfield of artificial intelligence (AI) and computer vision that focuses on the development of algorithms and models to enable computers to identify and categorize objects or entities within digital images or video frames. The goal of AI object recognition is to replicate and automate the human ability to visually perceive and understand the contents of images and videos.
